About Me

Hello! My name is Krishana Parikh

As a UX/UI generalist with a Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, I bring a unique combination of design and business acumen to my work. I have a passion for creating intuitive, user-centered designs through prototyping and user research. My approach to design is informed by data and user feedback, and I am dedicated to making design accessible and inclusive for all users.

My Tools

Experience in a range of design tools and software, including Sketch, Figma, Miro, InVesion and Adobe Creative Suite, as well as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for prototyping and developing functional prototypes, I am confident in my ability to deliver high-quality designs that meet both business goals and user needs. I am well-versed in Agile methodology and have experience using tools such as Trello to manage projects and collaborate with cross-functional teams. I am a strong communicator and collaborator, and enjoy working in fast-paced environments to bring designs to life.
